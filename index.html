About the Lab

The T6SS Laboratory focuses on agricultural and environmental microbiology, with particular emphasis on plant–microbe interactions, soilborne disease suppression, and beneficial microbial community engineering.

Research Highlights

  • Plant–microbe and microbe–microbe interactions
  • Rhizosphere and endophytic microbiomes
  • Microbial biocontrol and synthetic communities
  • Sustainable strategies for soilborne disease management
