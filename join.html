Open Positions

Graduate Students

We welcome highly motivated MSc and PhD applicants interested in agricultural and environmental microbiology. Applicants with experience in microbiology, bioinformatics, plant pathology, or multi-omics are encouraged to apply.

  • Topics: plant–microbe interactions, soilborne disease suppression, SynCom design, multi-omics integration
  • Preferred skills: microbial isolation, molecular biology, sequencing analysis, R/Python (optional)
  • How to apply: send a short CV and a brief statement of interest via email

Visiting Students / Interns

Visiting students and research interns are welcome. Please include your available dates, interests, and relevant experience.

Collaboration

We are open to collaborations with academic and industry partners on microbial resources, functional microbiome analysis, and translation to sustainable crop protection solutions.
